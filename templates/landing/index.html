Triage classification

Every second counts.

Healix categorizes patients into four urgency levels based on ESI protocols, giving your team instant clarity.

Critical
< 1min
Response window
Life-threatening. Immediate physician intervention required. Examples: cardiac arrest, severe respiratory distress, major trauma.
High
10min
Response window
Potentially life-threatening. Rapid assessment required. Examples: chest pain, altered mental status, severe pain.
Medium
30min
Response window
Urgent but stable. Can tolerate a brief wait. Examples: moderate pain, vomiting, minor fractures.
Low
2hr
Response window
Non-urgent. Routine care. Examples: minor cuts, mild cold, prescription refill, follow-up.
